Chapter Working Norms

Every chapter will have 5 founding members, all founding members are appointed by the prior approval of the national body.

Every chapter founding principal ( the first member who initiates the formation of the chapter ) will always be the representative of that chapter at the state level. It's the founding principal who brings together other founding members and form the chapter. All founding members unanimously approve every member in that chapter.

The membership fee collected will be shared in the following way – 60% with the chapter,20% with the state,10% with the region and 10% with the national body. All membership fees will be paid online and will be shared back by the national body. The founding members have the independence to spend the membership fees collected at their chapters. All the other revenue collected by the chapter will be completely by their discrepancies. 

  • Every chapter should achieve a 50 member enrolment in first 12 months from the date of formation.
  • Chapters will meet once in a month or twice as per their norms, but it's 12 chapter meetings mandatory per annum.
  • Chapters can conduct their own meetings and events as per their comfortable dates.
  • Members will elect the chapter president and office bearers every year and its mandatory none of the member can hold the office for more than 2 years consistently.
  • Every elected executive team ember is solely responsible in running the chapter  for the time of one year.
  • Every chapter will have a finance committee of 3 members with one founding member as a head of the committee at any point of time this committee will be the sole responsible for handling and making all financial decision.
  • Every chapter member is allowed to attend the other chapter meetings with prior approval from the respective chapters.

State Working Norms

It's the founding principal who brings together other founding members and form the state.

All the membership fee collected will be shared in the following way –

60% with the chapter,20% with the state,10% with the region and 10% with the national body. All membership fees will be paid online and it will be shared back by the national body. The founding member have the independence to spend the membership fees collected from their chapters in the state.

All the other revenue collected by the state will be completely by their discrepancies.

  • Every chapter should achieve a 50 member enrolment in first 12 months from the date of formation and the state will provide the,all necessary help by connecting them with the other chapters.
  • State will meet once in a month but it's 12 state  meetings mandatory per annum. The state will also take the lead to conduct the yearly state convention .
  • State can conduct their own meetings and events as per their comfortable dates .
  • Members will elect the state president and office bearers every year and its mandatory none of the member can hold the office for more than 2 years consistently.

  • Every state member is allowed to attend the other chapter meeting with prior approval from the respective chapters.

It's the founding principal who bring together other founding members and form the region. All founding members unanimously approve every member in that state chapter  formation , they also have the right to vote to dissolve or abandon the state chapters under any disciplinary action of the state chapter members .

60% with the chapter,20% with the state,10% with the region and 10% with the national body.
