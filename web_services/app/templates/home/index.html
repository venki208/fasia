Who we are ?

A society for Indo-American advisors, by advisors committed to protect and grow their lifetime assets of earnings, education, employment, equality and stand as a guard to help everyone in wealth creation and protection.

What We Do ?

OUR VISION

To be the best and the most preferred advisor community to everyone

OUR MISSION

  • Bringing together - To bring together all Indo American Financial Advisors and allied professionals as a community and enable them to be a global advisor
  • Make stronger - Increase the strength of the Indo American advisory community by 100% in next 3 years purely by a community support system
  • Grow their business - Create accessibility to cross border financial products and the knowledge through a legitimate and trustworthy channel and regulatory bodies
  • Enrich Culture - Provide a forum for FASIA members to enrich their cultural, social, and educational value
  • Enable Education - Help the members through Continuing Advisory Education (CAE) to maintain their high level of Advisory knowledge in specific Fields.

COMMITMENT

  • Foster the professional relationships among Indo American and other minority professionals working in the financial services industry.
  • Committed to strive continuously to expose its members to cutting edge trends and opportunities which will assist them in advancing their businesses and careers.
  • Committed to take the leadership and advocacy in connecting those who provide, support and benefit from professional financial planning.
  • We will demonstrate and support a professional commitment to eradicating financial illiteracy via educational initiatives and a client-centered financial planning process.
  • Committed to help aspiring Indo American to be an entrepreneur in financial domain as an advisor.

Your participation

  • FASIA is a non-profit organization.
  • First 2000 membership is free, only a registration fee of 25 USD per person.
  • Inviting founding members at city level to open chapters.
  • Institutions and Academics are Invited to apply for founding membership.
